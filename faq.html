We’re compiling the most common questions and clear, non-technical answers. In the meantime, here are a few quick ones:

Is HHM “just AI”?

No. HHM is a measurement framework. Operators, thresholds, nulls, and CIs are declared in the bundle and validated on data. An AI can help run it, but the numbers are not guesswork.

Can I run HHM without coding?

Yes. Start with How to Use HHM. You can load HHM_bundle.json into an AI that supports files, or use the provided container later for full reproducibility.

Where do I find datasets?

See Dataset Sheets & Provenance for examples and links. We also maintain a public list in the tests page.
